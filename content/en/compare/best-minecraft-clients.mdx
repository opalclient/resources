## Opal Client — Best Value

**Price:** $8.99 lifetime | **Type:** Hybrid ghost + blatant

Opal is the newest serious contender and it's making waves. At $8.99, it's the cheapest premium client on the market — but cheap doesn't mean lacking. 200+ features, server bypasses, ghost features for closet play, a GraalVM scripting engine, and a full platform with launcher, dashboard, config sharing, and community forums.

The hybrid approach means you don't need separate clients for different playstyles. Go blatant on popular servers or ghost on practice servers — one client handles both.

**Best for:** Players who want maximum value, hybrid playstyles, or a complete platform experience.

## Rise Client — Established Blatant

**Price:** $34.99 lifetime | **Type:** Blatant

Rise has been a staple in the blatant client space with 15,000+ customers. It offers solid server bypasses, multi-version support (1.8, 1.12, 1.19), and a scripting API. The community is large and active.

The downsides: no ghost features, no platform beyond Discord, and bypasses that reportedly lag behind updates. At $35, it's 4x the price of Opal for a narrower feature set.

**Best for:** Players who want a well-established blatant client with a large community and don't need ghost features.

## Vape V4 — Legacy Ghost

**Price:** $35 lifetime | **Type:** Ghost

Vape was the ghost client for years. Subtle aim assist, reach, velocity — the closet cheater's toolkit. It's injectable, meaning it works alongside Lunar Client and Badlion.

The problems: a 2.5/5 Trustpilot rating, frequent crashes, config sync issues, dismissive customer support, and no blatant features whatsoever. It's also $35 for ghost-only capability that Opal includes at $8.99.

**Best for:** Players who specifically need injectable compatibility with Lunar/Badlion and only want ghost features.

## Augustus — Module Maximalist

**Price:** ~$85 lifetime | **Type:** Blatant

Augustus boasts 240+ modules with 38,000+ settings — the most customizable client available. It bypasses anticheats like Intave, Polar, Grim, and Vulcan that other clients don't target.

The dealbreakers: **1.8.9 only**, ~$85 price tag, no ghost features, no platform, limited community. In 2026, paying $85 for a client locked to a decade-old Minecraft version is a hard sell.

**Best for:** Players on specific 1.8.9 servers with anticheats like Intave/Polar/Grim who want maximum customization.
